The Astro Cats will be teaching local high school students rocket physics, through the demonstration of water bottle rockets.
About the Astro Cats Educational Outreach
The Astro Cats will engage with over 200 high school students to teach basic physics, using water bottle rockets as a demonstration. The Astro Cats will give a 15 minute presentation, which will teach the students everything they need to know to successfully design and construct a water bottle rocket using provided materials. After the presentation, students will design and construct their own water bottle rockets in teams. Each team will be given a 2 liter bottle and an assortment of materials to construct the water bottle rocket. Once the construction is complete, a member of the Astro Cats will perform a safety inspection of the water bottle rocket. Students will then launch their wattle bottle rockets, and will be able to see a demonstration of rocket physics in action!
